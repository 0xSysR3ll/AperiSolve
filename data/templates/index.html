What is this ?

Aperi'Solve is an online platform which performs layer analysis on image. The platform also uses zsteg, steghide and exiftool for deeper steganography analysis. The platform supports the following images format: .png, .jpg, .gif, .bmp, .jpeg, .jfif, .jpe, .tiff...
