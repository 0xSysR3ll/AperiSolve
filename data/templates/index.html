Aperi'Solve

What is this ?

Aperi'Solve is an online platform which performs layer analysis on image. The platform also uses zsteg, steghide, exiftool, binwalk and strings for deeper steganography analysis. The platform supports the following images format: .png, .jpg, .gif, .bmp, .jpeg, .jfif, .jpe, .tiff...
